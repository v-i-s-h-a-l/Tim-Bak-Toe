### Description (4000 chars max)
Tim-Bak-Toe takes the classic game of tic-tac-toe and adds a twist that changes everything: each player gets exactly 3 pieces.

Place your pieces on the board, but choose carefully — once all 3 are down, you must move an existing piece to a new spot. No more filling up the board. No more guaranteed draws. Every move counts.

THE TWIST
Unlike regular tic-tac-toe where you keep placing new marks until the board fills up, Tim-Bak-Toe limits each player to 3 pieces. The game has two phases:

• Placement: Take turns putting your pieces on the board
• Relocation: Once all pieces are placed, move them to outmaneuver your opponent

This simple change transforms a game everyone knows into something genuinely strategic.

TIMED TURNS
Every turn is on a clock. Default is 5 seconds, but you can set it anywhere from 3 to 10 in settings. If you run out of time, you waste your turn. Four wasted turns and the game ends in a draw — but that almost never happens.

THREE WAYS TO PLAY
• Local: Pass and play with someone next to you
• vs AI: Three difficulty levels — Easy, Medium, and Hard. Hard uses a deep minimax search and won't go easy on you
• Online: Challenge anyone via Game Center matchmaking

DESIGNED WITH CARE
Neumorphic design with smooth animations, spring physics, drag-and-drop piece interaction, and adaptive layout that looks great on both iPhone and iPad. Supports light and dark mode.
